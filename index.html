ABOUT

As a student at Robert F. Kennedy High school and Make School Summer
Academy, I am enthusiastic about using technology for social good and
stretching our imagination to comprehend and make use of the discoveries in
the universe. I have interests in mobile and web development and have
completed programming courses. I have hosted a computer science club at my
school in teaching students how to make websites from scratch. I am also
involved in a non-profit organization called 'Nano Hacker Academy' that offers
advanced learning opportunities for tech-enthusiastic kids in the NYC metroarea.
